=== Emerge Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation Declaration ===

  1. Reporting content can be done within the app.
  2. Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) can be reported within the app.
  3. Users who have been reported as uploading CSAM will be banned from using any and all Emerge services indefinetly.
  4. When a report has been made of CSAM, the appropriate authorities will be contacted.
  5. Any and all CSAM material is removed instantly if the content has been found to have been uploaded by a user.
  6. Content may be randomly scanned for CSAM if the uploader has the same Internet Protocol (IP) address as a previous offender.
